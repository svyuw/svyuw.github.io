Reflection on Game Assignment

Overview

For this assignment, we were assigned to make a game using the p5 Play library.

To start off, I went through the practice activities to help me set up my own dev toolchain. Once I felt more comfortbale with setting the toolchain up, I also explored the p5 Play library and what capabilities it had. I discussed with my classmates potential game ideas and landed on the idea of having a cat chase mice around and score points.


My first step of making the game was to create a sprite and have this sprite be moveable. I wanted the sprite to move around with the arrow keys. I created the characters, the cat and the mouse, on Figma and made them into animations. These are that the animations look like:

Issues

This assignment was a bit difficult for me because it was my first time (in a long time) creating a dev toolchain and pushing something for deployment, so I was a bit confused with all the steps that went into it. However, after asking some friends and especially looking at the Discord, I was able to piece together the solution. I also struggled for a while with making the gifs show up and making the cat moveable. I went in for office hours and asked Reina for assistance with making the cat move, which was very helpful. My last issue that I had to work on was how to make the cat eat the mice, and ended up being able to use collideCircleCircle and putting that within the same forloop that would check if the mice is within the canvas.
